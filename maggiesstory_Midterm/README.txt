Creepy Camel - Katie Bixby, Emily Green, Madeline Grey, and Roxanne Low
Entertainment Software Design
Midterm presentation

Buttons throughout intro slides facilitate movement at your own pace. The space bar is the control for the animated text currently, so to view that feature, continue to press the space bar at your convenience.
Puzzle 1 has been made easier and a finished puzzle image has been added as a key. Puzzle 2 has been started, it features a drag-able bucket for catching falling bricks.
Empty the bucket (which will be animated to look like it's growing more full) after every five objects are collected by dumping the contents into the larger bucket.
